Truck Audit Instructions

  1. Enter your Technician Name and Contractor Firm / Garage.
  2. Tap Select Inventory File, then choose the file provided by the warehouse.
    • You will see “Inventory loaded” along with the total expected serials.
    • All expected items will appear in the Missing list.
  3. Tap the blue Scan button to begin scanning.
    • Aim the red line directly at the barcode or QR code.
    • Hold steady until the scan completes.
    • The scan will appear in the Last Scanned box.
    • Tap Scan again to confirm and move it into the results list.
    • Tap Finished when you are done scanning.
  4. As you scan:
    • Found = items successfully scanned from the list
    • Missing = items not scanned
    • Extra = items not on the inventory list
  5. Tap Copy Email so the address is ready to paste into the To: field.
  6. Tap Export Audit Results to generate your audit file.
    • If the Share sheet appears, choose Email (or Mail).
    • If a file preview appears, tap Share or More…, then choose Email.
  7. Paste the email address into the To: field.
  8. Send the email with the attached audit file.
